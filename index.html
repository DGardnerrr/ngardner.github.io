Biography

My name is Deanna Gardner. Software Engineering is one particular career that I aspire to get into. I am a first generation college student with a major in Business with a minor in Computer Science, currently in my 4th year at Brooklyn College. My family is originally from South America, but they immigrated here for better opportunities. A fun fact about me is that I have experience in speaking spanish & chinese. My native language is english.I enjoy doing community service charity work in my spare time & traveling.
